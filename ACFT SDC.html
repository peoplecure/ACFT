In SDC event, the personnel conducts 50-meter shuttles for time in five areas: sprint, drag, lateral, carry and sprint. The SDC is a test of strength, endurance, and anaerobic capacity, which are needed to accomplish high intensity combat tasks that last from a few seconds to several minutes.
