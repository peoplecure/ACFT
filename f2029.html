APAT Calculator




Applicant Physical Abilities Test (APAT) is the applicant fitness standard for the U.S. Secret Service. APAT evaluates applicant fitness for endurance, strength, and aerobic capacity. APAT is comprised of four components: push-ups, sit-ups, chin-ups, and a 1.5 mile run.

When U.S. Secret Service recruits arrive to training at James J. Rowley Training Center (JJRTC), the Secret Service measures recruits at the beginning, during, and end of training.
