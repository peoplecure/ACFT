In PLK event, the personnel maintains a proper plank position for as long as possible. The LTK assesses the strength of the Soldiers grip, arm, shoulder and trunk muscles. These muscles assist Soldiers in load carriage and in avoiding injuries to the back. The PLK is an alternate assessment that may be used. The plank helps build core strength that promotes back health and helps reduce injuries.
